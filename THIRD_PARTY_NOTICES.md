## Hartigan's Dip Test

**Original Algorithm:** Algorithm AS 217, Applied Statistics (1985) Vol. 34, No. 3, pp. 322-325

**MATLAB Translation:** F. Mechler, August 27, 2002

**Files:**
- `HartigansDipTest.m` - Direct translation from original FORTRAN code
- `HartigansDipSignifTest.m` - Significance testing wrapper

**Reference:**
> Hartigan, J. A., & Hartigan, P. M. (1985). The Dip Test of Unimodality.
> The Annals of Statistics, 13(1), 70-84.
